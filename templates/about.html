About SpotifY2K

SpotifY2K is an open source website that displays a user's top tracks and artists in a retro inspired aesthetic.

What do the numbers mean in the headers of top artists and tracks?

The number highest to the logo is your most played - 1 is your most played and 10 is your 10th most played.

My list doesn't seem right, are you sure your data is correct?

SpotifY2K uses data collected by Spotify in the past month, 6 months, and one year. If your list looks strange, there might be a slight offset in how Spotify collects data, or it might be a song or artist you listened to a long time ago!
